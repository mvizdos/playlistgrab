Data Privacy Protocol

Privacy Policy

Short Version: PlaylistGrab does not collect, sell, or store your personal information. The links you paste are processed purely to extract video URLs and are forgotten immediately after processing.

How It Works

Whenever possible, playlist pages are fetched directly in your browser. If a proxy is required to bypass CORS restrictions, we use a stateless Cloudflare Worker. This worker forwards your request to YouTube and returns the HTML. It does not log, store, or analyze the data passing through it.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect IP addresses.
  • We do not store the playlists you fetch.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics pixels.
