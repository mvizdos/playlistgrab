Privacy Policy

Short version: PlaylistGrab does not collect or sell your personal information. The links you paste are processed to extract video URLs and are not used for any other purpose.

How it works: Whenever possible, playlist pages are fetched and parsed directly in your browser. If a cross-origin request is blocked, the site may use a short-lived proxy to fetch the playlist HTML. When a proxy is used, only the requested playlist HTML is forwarded; PlaylistGrab does not store personal data or keep long-term logs of the content fetched. Any proxy used is independent and subject to its own policies.

Data we do not collect: We do not collect names, email addresses, or playlist content beyond what you explicitly paste or request. We do not place tracking cookies or third‑party analytics scripts on this site. We use Google Search Console only to view aggregate site performance metrics (no visitor-level data).

Retention and removal: We do not retain playlist content or extracted links on the server. If you believe any data related to your use needs to be removed, contact us at hello@playlistgrab.com.

Children: This site is not intended for children under 13. Do not submit personal data of minors.

Questions or concerns? Contact: hello@playlistgrab.com
